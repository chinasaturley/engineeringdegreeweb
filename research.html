Research & Discovery

Our Mission

The mission of research for College of Engineering students is to cultivate a culture of innovation, exploration, and problem-solving. Through rigorous inquiry and hands-on experimentation, students are encouraged to push the boundaries of knowledge in their respective fields. Research serves as a vital platform for them to engage in real-world challenges, contribute to advancements in technology and science, and develop critical thinking skills. By fostering a collaborative environment and providing opportunities for mentorship, the College of Engineering empowers its students to develop groundbreaking solutions, drive meaningful change, and emerge as future leaders in their disciplines.
